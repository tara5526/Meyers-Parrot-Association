Who are we?

The Meyer's Parrot association is a group of people committed to providing care, information and advice for Meyer's parrots and their owners and breeders.


Why Meyer's Parrot Association?

The Meyer's Parrot Association provides care and information that is specific to the breed, as different breeds of birds have different requirements.
